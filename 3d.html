Welcome to a vast universe... where foreign, alienated creatures are nonexistent anymore... marked by the death of their last... being an abandoned language. Do you truly identify with the language you supposedly “speak”? When was the last time you jotted down letters and watched your ideas flow as ink on paper? Don’t you feel like words on the tip of your tongue are stuck in your throat like a fish bone, impossible to pull out or swallow?
